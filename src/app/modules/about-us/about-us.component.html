WHO WE ARE

To achieve harmony with land, we need to respect it and compliment it with a structure that does justice to its aura

GENERIC - the name gives us an identity as it defines our ability to look at things in an equal manner and gel with any element effortlessly. With this, we have kept a culture of assimilating client’s mindset & blending with their distinctive requirements, so that we can devise ideas that will make us grow along with our clients. Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited was incorporated in 1994. However, our journey has begun long before. ‘Bringing a significant difference in everyone’s life’ was a constant dream that our chairwenc ouragedasman Mr Ravilal Patel in 1965 tocd tread a path that undulating at the beginning. The initial days were wisely invested in setting up a solid foundation to withstand the promise that he made to the mankind. The early 80s witnessed long cherished dream taking shape with the company unfurling a list of quality projects in & around Mumbai & Navi Mumbai. The purpose was noble, the vision was clear & there was no looking back again. Now, it’s been over 5 decades, things have evolved, but the vision that we put forward still remains the same. GENERIC is a Public Limited Company which is indexed on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and National Stock Exchange (NSE).
